NATURE FOXY- About

With growing time and generation people have taken mental health for granted, with increasing population there is increase in pollution, shortage of utilities and heavy workload which cause extreme stress, anger, health issues and many more. To truncate these problems that cause vexation needs natures therapy of sounds that have been serving generations of people, to their best in everyday life that may be studying, creating, working and many more. So where are these sounds missing in the noise of busy roads, the railway wails or the busy life.
The webpage “Nature Foxy” is built using the HTML and CSS code which is a collection of various natural and relaxing sounds that would help people in making their minds stress-free and would also help in improving mental health and therapy for many psychiatric issues. It can also be useful in making the background sounds for various videos and purposes
